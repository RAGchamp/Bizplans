About the $175k Potential Figure

What "potential" means in these business plans, and what it doesn't.

In short: every net-profit figure shown on this site — including the $175k/year figure — is a modeled, assumption-driven estimate derived from a hypothetical scenario described in the relevant business plan. It is not a projection, promise, guarantee, or forecast of actual results. Nobody's income is guaranteed by reading, following, or attempting any plan published by RAGChamp.

1. The Figure Is Based on Stated Assumptions

Each business plan on this site lays out a specific chain of assumptions — for example, a certain number of downloads or leads, a certain conversion rate, a certain pricing structure, a certain cost budget, and a certain amount of operator time spent on distribution and product development. The $175k (or any other) net-profit figure is simply what falls out of that specific chain of assumptions if every one of them holds true.

Change any assumption — the conversion rate, the channel mix, the cost of acquisition, the tax rate, the timeline — and the resulting figure changes too, potentially to a much lower number, to zero, or to a loss. The plans themselves generally show unit economics and quarterly ramps precisely so a reader can see which assumptions are doing the most work, and how sensitive the final number is to each one.

2. The Figure Assumes Operator Skill and Effort That Isn't Provided

Every plan assumes execution by a solo operator who already has, or acquires, the business acumen and Agentic AI technical skills the plan calls for — including but not limited to: software development and testing (including effective use of AI coding tools), product design, pricing and packaging decisions, marketing and community distribution, sales and vendor negotiation, basic financial and tax literacy, and sound day-to-day business judgment.

RAGChamp does not provide this skill or judgment, does not vet or certify any reader's ability to execute a plan, and does not perform any part of the work described in any plan on a reader's behalf. The plans are written material only — they are not training, coaching, staffing, or a guarantee that a given individual is capable of the execution they describe.
